    Use your location or a postcode to find the nearest spaces

Why do you need my location?

To get the best accuracy for the spaces closest to you, location services gives us the most accurate data to help you find where to park.

I don't like using location services

That's no problem. If you would prefer not to use location services, you can enter a postcode and find the closest spaces to that area instead.

I found a space that isn't on the map

Great! If you have the details we can get it added to the map. Get in touch by sending an email or fill out the form below.

I went to the location and there was no space

Sorry about that. We try to update the map as often as possible. If this does happen, let us know and we will try and find out what is going on.

I got a parking ticket?!

This website is just a guide to where you can park. Please make sure you read all the signs before parking. We are not liable for your parking ticket.

Can I park for free at pay and display areas?

Yes! You can park in any existing on-street pay and display zone or any Aberdeen City Council car park as long as you display your blue badge.
